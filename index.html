UPRISING HUMANUM

Challenging Gameplay!

Permadeath at it's core, each hit matters, and reaching the top is the ultimate goal.

Having features like

Perma-Death

Make each try count, reaching the top is hard. And each time you are killed you will start from the very bottom. This may be hard but nobody said it will be easy.

Aware Story

The story itself is aware of the loop, and it's aware of your actions and choices.

Metroidvania

Each time you reach a new floor, you will learn a new secret of the tower, this will make you understand her better, and find new hidden locations and connections.
